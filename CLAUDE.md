# CLAUDE.md

## Project Overview

This is the personal website for Diego Arrieta (diegoarrieta.com). It is a static single-page site that serves as a living portfolio — a place for writing, projects, coaching, and creative work.

## Who is Diego?

Diego Arrieta is a creator, educator, and coach based in Mexico City. He is Uruguayan with a Peruvian mother, lived in California for 10 years, and now lives in Mexico. He is the co-founder of OZOM.ai, an AI transformation consultancy.

His core mission: **create products and educational content**. He helps people use AI to 10x their work, coaches executives on technology implementation, and writes about AI, product thinking, customer experience, and life across cultures.

He is also a guitarist, boxer, soccer player, tennis player, weightlifter, reader, and writer. The site should reflect all of these dimensions — not just the professional ones.

## Brand & Design Principles

- **Warm, editorial, personal** — not corporate, not tech-bro
- **Color palette**: Earth tones — clay (#C4956A), rust (#B85C3A), ocean (#2D5F6E), sage (#7A8B6F), ink (#1A1714), paper (#F5F0E8)
- **Typography**: Playfair Display (headings), Source Sans 3 (body), JetBrains Mono (labels/accents)
- **Tone**: Intelligent but approachable. Honest. Multicultural. Curious. Never salesy.
- **Feel**: Like a great independent magazine — thoughtful, spacious, alive

## Site Sections

- **Hero**: Personal intro with the line "I create, I teach, I follow curiosity wherever it goes"
- **/write**: Essays and long-form thinking (AI, product, culture, discipline, philosophy)
- **/build**: Current projects — OZOM.ai, courses, AI experiments, consulting work
- **/coach**: Executive coaching — application-based, limited availability, premium positioning
- **/learn**: Courses, cohorts, community (future)
- **/play**: Music, boxing, soccer, tennis, reading — the human side
- **/about**: The full story — Uruguay, Peru, California, Mexico City
- **Newsletter**: Weekly email on what Diego is learning

## Tech Details

- Pure static HTML/CSS/JS — no frameworks, no build step
- Single file (index.html) for now — will expand to multiple pages as content grows
- Hosted on GitHub Pages with custom domain
- Google Workspace handles email (diego@diegoarrieta.com)
- Fonts loaded from Google Fonts CDN
- CSS animations for scroll reveals and hover effects
- Fully responsive (desktop, tablet, mobile)

## Content Voice Guidelines

When writing content for this site:
- First person, conversational, honest
- Cross-disciplinary — connect ideas from boxing to business, music to management
- Never generic "AI tips" content — always filtered through Diego's unique perspective
- Spanish phrases or cultural references are welcome when natural
- Avoid corporate jargon, buzzwords, and LinkedIn-speak
- Write like you're talking to a smart friend, not presenting to a board

## Working on This Project

- Keep it simple. Resist the urge to over-engineer.
- Design changes should maintain the warm, editorial feel.
- Every section should feel alive and personal — if it could be on anyone's website, it shouldn't be on Diego's.
- When in doubt, ask: "Does this reflect who Diego actually is?"

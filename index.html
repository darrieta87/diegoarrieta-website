About

My Story

I grew up in Montevideo, with a Peruvian mother who taught me that the world is bigger than any one country. I spent a decade in Sacramento, California, where I learned product thinking, startup culture, and the power of building things people actually want. Now I live in Morelia, Michoacán, where I'm building a life around the things I love most: creating, teaching, and following my curiosity wherever it leads.

I've been a product manager, a commercial director, a soccer coach, a consultant, and a dozen other things. The thread that connects all of it is this: I take complex ideas, make them clear, and help people transform.
